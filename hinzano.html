Kennedy Hinzano is currently a fourth year student at the Technical University of Kenya pursuing Bachelor of Commerce (Finance Option).

He is the current serving Secretary General of the Finance Students Association (FISA) which is an association embedded with the core objective of connecting comrades to the industry in accounting and finance fields.

He is also an active member of Association of Business Students (ABS). He is also an active member of the Christian Union of Technical University and has served as the Vice-Coordinator and acting Treasurer of Creative Ministry which is an extension of the Missions and Evangelism Committee of the Christian Union in the spiritual year 2015/2016. He is also interested in entrepreneurship and is the CEO and Founder of HinKen Enterprises Ltd which is a small medium enterprise that is involved in the sale and distribution of second hand clothes to towns and villages without the reach of the Capital Nairobi.

He is also interested in mentorship of young people in high school and primary school and this has made him to be part of the Student Beyond Classroom (SBC) group which has mentorship as one of its core objective.

He has also interest in Akad Education Africa Group which provides mentorship to students of all levels and through Akad Group trainings he has and still is being equipped with mentorship skills and also being mentored in the process in order to also add the same value to others.
